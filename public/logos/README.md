## How to Replace Logos

### Option 1: Download from Official Sources
1. Visit each company's official website
2. Look for their media/press kit or brand assets page
3. Download official logos (preferably SVG or high-resolution PNG)
4. Save them with the same filename in the appropriate directory

### Option 2: Request from Companies
Contact the marketing/communications department of each company and request:
- Official logo files
- Brand usage guidelines
- Permission to use their logo on your platform

## Recommended Logo Specifications

- **Format**: SVG (preferred) or PNG with transparent background
- **Size**: At least 200x200 pixels for PNG
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square or close to square works best
- **File Size**: Keep under 100KB for optimal performance

## Where Logos are Used

These logos are displayed in:
1. **Landing Page - Services Section**: Shows all available services with their provider logos
2. **Landing Page - Banking Partners Section**: Displays partner bank logos
